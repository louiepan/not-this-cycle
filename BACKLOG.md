Likely lives in `EventScheduler.materializeEvent` (track last-presented decision time, defer subsequent presentations into a queue). Overwhelm is the satire; collision is not.

Welcome to Plinth." Sets the contract without breaking immersion. Author in `q4-planning.ts` as an `evt-onboarding-rules` event with triggerAt: 1000.
- [ ] **End-game "moments that mattered" section** — On the review screen, surface 2-3 specific decisions where the player's text matched, formatted as: "When Elena asked for dates → you said 'I'll get you the dates tonight' → Elena now thinks you over-commit. - [ ] **End-game "things you didn't say" callouts** — For the 1-2 most impactful pure-timeout decisions (matchSource: 'timeout'), surface what the moment was and frame the cost of silence. Don't reveal authored alternatives. Keeps the satire intact while making the loss feel earned.
- [ ] **Tone trajectory visual on review screen** — A simple sparkline or pill row showing the player's tone across decisions ("deflecting, deflecting, committing, deflecting…"). Lets the player see their own pattern, not just outcomes. Source: `resolvedDecisions[].matchedTone` (already captured).
